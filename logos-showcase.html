Option 1 — Mark + Wordmark

Double-nested diamond with a center dot. The diamond signals precision and focus — a fixed point in strategic ambiguity. Works at small sizes (favicon, watermark on decks). The vertical separator creates clear hierarchy between mark and name.

Option 2 — Typographic

Large italic "Sai" in gold anchors the mark — personal, warm, human. "BHARADWAJ" in tracked small caps below provides formal weight and contrast. No icon needed. Best for document headers, email signatures, and contexts where the name should stand alone.
